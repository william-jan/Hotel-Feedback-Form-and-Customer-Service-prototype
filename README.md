# Hotel-Feedback-Form-and-Customer-Service-prototype

This is a **prototype** for a hotel *feedback form* and *customer service* chatbot

There are 2 pages:
1. The *feedback form* page
2. The *customer service* chatbot page

The form is a simple HTML form that asks for the following information:
- Name
- Email
- Age
- Gender
- Was this your first time at our hotel?
- How was your stay?
- Why did you choose to stay at our hotel?
- What did you like about our hotel?
- What did you not like about our hotel?
- Any suggestions for improvement?

The chatbot is a simple chatbot that answers questions about the hotel and its services.

The prototype is a simple prototype that is not intended for production use.
